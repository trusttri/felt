Welcome to the Felt.dev consentful onboarding sketch!

This sketch is a proof of concept, side-by-side comparsion between two onboarding flows:

  1. one column uses dark design patterns that create an unconsentful experience
  2. the other column is designed with the affirmative consent framework described by consentful.systems

The goal here is to compare and contrast the two perspectives. We hope you feel the difference!
